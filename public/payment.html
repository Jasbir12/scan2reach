FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

How does the QR code work?
When someone scans your QR code with their phone camera, they'll be taken to your digital profile page where they can see your contact information, social links, and save your contact directly.
Can I edit my profile after creating it?
Yes! You can edit your profile details anytime from your dashboard. All changes are reflected instantly. Your QR code itself never changes — it always points to your live profile.
What happens after the subscription ends?
We'll send you a reminder before your subscription expires. If you don't renew, your profile will be deactivated (but not deleted) until you renew. Your data is always safe.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure Firebase infrastructure. Only the information you choose to share is visible on your public profile.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all digital plans. For physical card plans, refunds are available before the card has been shipped.
